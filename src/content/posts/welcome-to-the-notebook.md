I wanted a place that felt slower than social media and more flexible than a notes app.

This site is where I can leave thoughts, questions, and half-finished ideas in public. Some posts will be polished. Some will just be honest.

Over time, I want the archive itself to become useful: a record of what kept returning to my mind, what changed, and what was worth keeping.
